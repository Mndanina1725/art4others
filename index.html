About Us

Art for Others is a student-led, non-profit organization dedicated to uniting communities through the power of creative ingenuity. Based in Regina and Saskatoon Saskatchewan, we offer a range of visual arts programs designed to inspire connection and inclusivity. Our initiatives bring together individuals from all backgrounds, creating meaningful relationships while promoting mental health and overall well-being.

Get to know the Team

Angelena Wu

Co-Founder

Angelena grew up in Regina, Saskatchewan. Passionate about mental health advocacy, Angelena started Art for Others to inspire through creative expression. When she’s not studying, you’ll probably find her painting, hitting the gym with friends or rock climbing (which mostly involves her trying not to fall off the wall).

Mission

At Art for Others, our mission is to harness the impact of art and creative expression to connect and include diverse communities of all ages across Saskatchewan, fostering unity and shared understanding.
