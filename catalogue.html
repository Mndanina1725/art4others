Our Catalogue

Youth programs

At Art4Others, we currently hold weekly visits to Ronald McDonald House where our volounteers spend meaningful time with children and their families through arts, crafts, and creative activities . Each session is rooted in connection and joy which helps to create moments of laughter. Art4Others has collaborated with organizations such as BestBuddies Saskatoon, Regina Public Libraries and others, that work to continue upholding our initiative.

Senior care and rehabilitation programs

Our team’s partnership with the Saskatchewan Health Authority has allowed us to host programs at the Wascana Rehabilitation Centre, in Regina. There, many of the residents who may experience a disconnect from the outside world, made many meaningful memories with our volunteers!

Fundraisers and Community Support Initiatives

Through a variety of community-driven initiatives, our team has worked to support our mission and give back in meaningful ways. From bottle drives, fine arts nights with live music, and Canada Day events, each effort is rooted in service and connection. We have also proudly donated products to the Young Women’s Christian Association (YWCA) in Saskatoon and continue to look for new ways to uplift and support our community.
